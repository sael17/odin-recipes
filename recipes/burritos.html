Mexican Style Homemade Burritos

Burritos

This Mexican dish contains ground beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, cream, tacos and empanadas. The beef is usually seasoned.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Cream
  • Tacos
  • Empanadas

    • Steps

    1. Cook the ground beef
    2. Prepare the lettuce
    3. Prepare the tomatoes
    4. Prepare the cheese
    5. Prepare the cream
    6. Prepare the tacos
    7. Prepare the empanadas
