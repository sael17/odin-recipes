Yellow Rice with Beans, Turkey, pasteles and potato salad

Dish #1

This Puerto Rican dish contains yellow rice with beans, roasted turkey, pasteles, and a selection of potato, macaroni or green salads. The turkey is usually seasoned.

Ingredients

  • Yellow rice
  • Beans
  • Roasted turkey
  • Pasteles
  • Potato salad

Steps

  1. Prepare the yellow rice with beans
  2. Roast the turkey
  3. Prepare the pasteles
  4. Prepare the potato salad
